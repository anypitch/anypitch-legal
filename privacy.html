Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

AnyPitch (“the app”, “we”) is an offline, on-device singing-practice app. It is designed so that your data never leaves your phone. By default the app collects nothing; the only exceptions are optional features you control, described below.

1 Data we collect

By default: none. AnyPitch has no user accounts, no sign-in, no advertising, and no tracking SDKs. We do not collect, transmit, sell, rent, or share any personal information with us or any third party.

Optional anonymous usage statistics — only if you turn them on. If you enable “Share anonymous usage statistics” (offered at the end of the introduction and available anytime in Settings, off by default), the app sends us anonymous events about feature usage: for example “a song was imported”, “a practice session was completed”, roughly how long processing took, which optional settings are in use, coarse device characteristics (model, OS version, processor core count, memory size, display refresh rate, a rough measure of how smoothly the app runs), and the app version. These events contain no account, no name, no email, no advertising ID, no device identifier, no location, no audio, no song titles and no file names, and cannot be linked to you. They are processed by Aptabase, a privacy-first analytics service that stores only aggregate statistics and uses no cookies, fingerprinting, or long-term identifiers. You can turn this off at any time in Settings, and it stays off after updates.

2 Microphone

When you practice, AnyPitch uses your device microphone to analyze your singing pitch in real time, entirely on your device. AnyPitch does not create a voiceprint, does not identify or authenticate you, and never records, stores, or transmits your voice. The audio is processed transiently in memory and discarded.

3 Your audio files and generated content

Songs you import, and any vocal or instrumental stems, notes, or lyrics AnyPitch generates from them, are stored only in the app’s private storage on your device. They are never uploaded, and AnyPitch does not use Android’s cloud backup, so this content is not copied to Google Drive or any other cloud service. It is deleted when you delete the song or uninstall the app. Files you choose to export (.anypitch) are written only to the location you pick.

4 Network use

AnyPitch uses the network only for three things, all under your control:

  • A one-time, user-initiated download of its AI models from Google Play’s content-delivery service when you first set up the app. No personal information about you is sent during this download.
  • The optional anonymous usage statistics described in Section 1, only if you enabled them.
  • Feedback you choose to send from Settings (Section 8).

Everything else works fully offline. If Google Play’s optional in-app rating card ever appears, it is provided and handled entirely by Google Play under Google’s own privacy policy; AnyPitch only asks Play to show it and receives nothing back.

AnyPitch itself never sends your personal information to us or to anyone, and we operate no server that receives it. Separately, Google Play — the app store that distributes AnyPitch and delivers the models — may collect standard installation and diagnostic information under Google’s own privacy policy. That collection is Google’s, not ours, and is outside our control; AnyPitch provides Google no personal information about you.

5 Permissions we request and why

  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO) — to analyze your singing pitch in real time on your device (Section 2).
  • Read audio / media files — only to let you pick a song you choose to import.
  • Notifications — to show progress while the app processes a song in the background.
  • Internet — only for the one-time AI-model download, the optional anonymous usage statistics if you enabled them, and feedback you choose to send (Section 4).

AnyPitch may also offer an optional shortcut to your device’s battery optimization settings. If you choose to open it, you can decide whether to exclude AnyPitch from battery optimization so long song processing is less likely to be interrupted. This is optional, handled by Android system settings, and does not send any personal information to us.

We do not request location, contacts, camera, or any advertising identifier.

6 Children

AnyPitch is intended for users aged 13 and older and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children (and in fact collect no personal information from anyone; the optional usage statistics in Section 1 are anonymous).

7 Your rights

Because AnyPitch holds no personal data about you on any server, there is nothing for us to access, correct, export, or delete on your behalf. You remain in full control of the files stored on your device and can delete them in the app or by uninstalling. The optional usage statistics are anonymous, so they cannot be looked up, linked to you, or deleted per person; you can stop them at any time in Settings. If you emailed us, you can ask us to delete that correspondence (Section 8).

8 Contact & feedback

If you email us (at the address below), we receive your email address and the content of your message. We use them only to read and respond to you, never share or sell them, and delete the correspondence when it is no longer needed.

The in-app “Send Feedback” form (in Settings) is anonymous: it sends only the message you type, plus the app version and your device model and OS version so bugs can be reproduced. It is delivered to our inbox through Web3Forms, a form-forwarding service, under its own privacy policy. Like any internet request, Web3Forms receives standard technical data such as your IP address while transporting the message and may include it in the delivery email; AnyPitch does not add it, and we do not use or store it. We cannot identify or reply to you unless you choose to include an email address in the form, so please do not put personal information in the message. The same applies to the feedback form on this website.

9 Security & third-party platforms

AnyPitch is designed to keep your data on your device and to send nothing to us. However, no app, device, or method of electronic storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure, and we cannot promise absolute security. Services and platforms we do not control — including Google Play, your device’s operating system and manufacturer software, and any cloud, backup, sync, or sharing settings you enable on your device or accounts — operate under their own terms and privacy policies. We are not responsible for how those third parties or your own device and accounts handle data outside AnyPitch, and your use of the app is at your own risk (see the Terms of Use).

10 Changes to this policy

If we change how the app works, we will update this policy and the “Last updated” date above.
